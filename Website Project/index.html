My Favorite Warhammer Chapters

My favorite warhammer chapters are the Salamanders, the Iron hands, and the Flesh Tearers. The reason I like them so much is because they're truly different than any other chapters, and really represent the underdogs of the Imperium of Man.

Salamanders

The Salamanders hail from the buning planet Nocturne. The reason the Salamanders are my favorite is becuase they are truly the most emapthetic bunch in the Imperium. They also suffer some of the most casualities in order to save lives, which grants them the underdog status in my book.

Iron Hands

The Iron Hands embody tragedy, being that their Primarch was the first to die during the Horus Heresy. Being split into smaller clans during and after the heresy, they struggle to gain a solid foundation and often go underlooked by many Warhammer fans.

Flesh Tearers

What I like about the Flesh Tearers is how different they are from their founding chapter. The Flesh Tearers are a successor chapter to the Blood Angels. What sets them apart from their founder and their brother successor chapters, is that they're ruthless even when they aren't affected by the black rage.
